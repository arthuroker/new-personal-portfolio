Most people try to brute-force discipline.

A better strategy is to design your default environment so good actions are easier than bad ones.

- Put your writing file in the same place every day.
- Keep your daily publishing checklist short.
- Measure streaks by sessions completed, not mood.

A system does not need to be complex. It only needs to make the next right step obvious.
